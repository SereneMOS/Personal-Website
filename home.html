About Me
Hello, my name is Rosaline Flowers, and this is my personal website, or my Garden! I've been working on it since June 2024 as a place to store my odd sundries and projects; feel free to look around at my stuff, maybe find some secrets. In case you're curious, the lady on the right is me! In my mind, every day is an occasion worth dressing up for, and these are among my favorite outfits. I grew up in Elizabethtown, Kentucky, but now live in Rochester, New York, and if you'd like to learn more about where I've been, I'd recommend the Travel Map.

Education
